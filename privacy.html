Blu Studio — Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 6, 2026 · Provided by BLU Productions

Blu Studio is a professional tool used by authorized dealership staff to photograph vehicle inventory and send those photos to their dealership's own systems. This policy explains what the app accesses and why.

What the app accesses

  • Camera — to take photos and 360° spins of vehicles. Photos are captured only when the user chooses to.
  • Vehicle information you enter — such as stock number or VIN, used to label and file the photos correctly.
  • A store access code — used once to activate the app for a specific dealership.

How that information is used

  • Vehicle photos and their labels are uploaded to the dealership's designated inventory storage and listing feed (for example, the dealer's photo hosting and vAuto), so they appear on that dealer's vehicle listings.
  • The information is used solely to deliver the dealership's own photos to the dealership's own systems.

What we do NOT do

  • We do not sell or rent your information to anyone.
  • We do not use the photos or data for advertising.
  • The app contains no third-party advertising or tracking SDKs.
  • We do not collect location, contacts, or personal photos from your library.

Data storage & retention

Photos are transmitted over encrypted connections (HTTPS) to the dealership's designated storage and feed. Blu Studio does not retain a separate copy beyond what is needed to complete an upload. The dealership controls the photos in its own systems.

Who can use the app

Blu Studio is intended for authorized staff of participating dealerships and requires a store access code to function. It is not intended for the general public.
